Tracking

This entire website does NOT use Facebook Social Plugins, Pixels, or Twitter Share buttons.

The only tracking tool that is being used is the locally installed Matomo Analytics tool which in addition, any IP address or personal identifier is made anonymous, and is also setup not not to use any cookies. You can learn more about what Matomo can track here.

Sometimes there is embedded content which might have tracking based on the service used (YouTube or Instagram most notably).

I am running a server in the Netherlands and their logs which may include your IP address but this information is not further processed in any way.

The hosting company used for this website is Scaleway and for faster loading and site security I also use CloudFlare. Both of these service providers are GDPR compliant and may have access to your IP address and other data.

The website runs on 11ty, a static site generator.
