Instagram Paragraph Whitespaces

Tutorial Video on YouTube

Making paragraphs work on Instagram is a pain. You can't have whitespaces at the end of any line, empty lines have to have some kind of character (otherwise they get removed), and it's really easy to mess these things up.

This mini tool automatically removes the whitespaces at the end of each line and puts invisble characters into the empty lines. This way you have nice looking paragraphs! #YAY

Click "Make Whitespaces" to transform.
